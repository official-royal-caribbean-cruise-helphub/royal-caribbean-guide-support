Planning your +1-855-690-1232 Royal Caribbean cruise? Here are 11 smart, proven strategies to help you get the most out of your drink packages, without overspending:

1. Book In Advance

Use the Cruise Planner online tool to buy drink packages before boarding — they’re often 30-40% cheaper online.

2. Watch For Promotions

Look out for frequent promotions and bundle offers during sales like Black Friday or Cruise Planner sales.

3. Do The Math

Estimate how many drinks you’ll realistically consume per day — often 5-7 drinks/day makes the package worth it.

4. Compare Packages

Choose between the Deluxe Beverage Package, Refreshment Package, or Soda Package depending on your habits.

5. Avoid Last-Minute Purchases

Buying onboard is more expensive. Plan ahead to save big.

6. Mix & Match

One person in the cabin can buy a package, while others can go à la carte if not heavy drinkers.

7. Use Loyalty Points

Royal Caribbean Crown & Anchor members may get discounts or free drinks depending on their tier.

8. Know What’s Included

Drink packages may cover bottled water, specialty coffee, juice, and mocktails — not just alcohol.

9. Avoid Sharing

Sharing drink packages is not allowed and may result in cancellation without refund.

10. Leverage Group Discounts

Sometimes group bookings through travel agents can get bulk deals on drink packages.

11. Ask Guest Services

If unsure, call or chat with Royal Caribbean support to get updated info about ongoing discounts or offers.
